Whether you see swimming as relaxation, fun or exercise, here at Splash Pool we can accomodate your needs. Our certified aquatic instructors oversee all family, kiddie, and public swims and we offer many times during the day for people of all ages to enjoy our pool.

Our pool is saltwater and heated at a minimum temperature of seventy five degrees. First time clients are welcome to a complimentary swim and will enjoy our steam rooms, saunas and state of the art shower facilities. The pool is Olympic sized, measuring 50 metres by 18 metres. Also for your enjoyment are kickboards,noodles and lifejackets, available to borrow free of charge.

Come see what you've been missing, and let's get to swimming!
